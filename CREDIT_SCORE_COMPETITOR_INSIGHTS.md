## Differentiation Thesis

**Nobody in West Africa scores credit from informal savings group participation.**

Sika Family Vault is uniquely positioned because:
1. We already have the Susu circle infrastructure (MVP built)
2. We capture all the behavioral data (contribution consistency, amounts, cycles, defaults)
3. MTN MoMo + Vodafone Cash integration means real transactional data
4. Family sub-accounts add household financial behavior context

TransUnion Ghana + MTN SA's model validates the approach: telco data + savings behavior = predictive credit score.
